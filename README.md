## Conversion Optimization Strategy

### Primary Goals
1. Generate phone calls (primary conversion)
2. Capture leads through contact form (secondary conversion)
3. Build trust and credibility

### Tactics Implemented
1. **Prominent Phone Numbers** - In header, hero, footer, and throughout content
2. **Multiple CTAs** - "Get Free Estimate" and "Call Now" buttons on every page
3. **Emergency Service Emphasis** - 24/7 availability highlighted
4. **Trust Building** - Certifications, warranties, years in business, BBB rating
5. **Service Area Focus** - Clear geographic coverage to attract local searches
6. **Social Proof** - Statistics (5,000+ projects, 15+ years, 100% satisfaction)
7. **Comprehensive FAQ** - Addresses common objections and questions
8. **Easy Contact** - Multiple ways to reach out (phone, email, form)
